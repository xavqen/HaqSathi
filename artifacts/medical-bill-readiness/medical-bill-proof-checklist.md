# Medical bill dispute proof checklist

- Final bill and itemized bill breakup
- Estimate/package quote/rate card if available
- Payment receipt, deposit receipt and transaction proof
- Discharge/test/service proof or prescription where relevant
- Insurance/TPA pre-auth, denial, deduction or claim status proof
- Billing desk/support communication trail
- Redacted sharing copy with OTP/CVV/UPI PIN/password/full health ID/full policy number hidden
