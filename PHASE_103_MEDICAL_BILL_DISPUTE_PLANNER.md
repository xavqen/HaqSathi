# Phase 103 - Medical Bill Dispute Planner

Added a practical user-facing tool for hospital, clinic, diagnostic lab, pharmacy, ambulance and insurance/TPA billing disputes.

## Added

- `/tools/medical-bill-dispute-planner`
- `MedicalBillDisputePlannerForm`
- Billing dispute plan generator
- Itemized bill/proof checklist
- Insurance/TPA escalation route
- Copy-ready billing clarification message
- Medical + payment privacy safety warnings
- `/admin/medical-bill-readiness`
- `/api/admin/medical-bill-readiness`
- `npm run medical-bill:readiness`
- `npm run phase103:audit`

## Safety boundary

This workflow is guidance only. It does not provide medical, legal, insurance or financial advice and must not ask for OTP, CVV, UPI PIN, password, full health ID, full policy number or excessive medical history.
